Siphosethu Krakra (Founder & Head Chef)

About Siphosethu Krakra

With over a decade in the culinary industry, Siphosethu Krakra is the creative force behind "In The Kitchen With Sethu". Born and raised in Soweto, Siphosethu grew up savoring the rich flavors and diverse dishes of South Africa. Her journey began in her grandmother’s kitchen, where she learned that food is not just about taste, but about culture, connection, and storytelling.

After formal training in culinary arts and years of honing her craft in renowned kitchens, Siphosethu returned home to bring her vision to life. Her cooking philosophy is simple: use fresh, locally sourced ingredients to create dishes that honor tradition while adding a modern twist.

Why Choose In The Kitchen With Sethu?

  • Authentic South African recipes passed down through generations
  • Inventive fusion dishes inspired by Soweto’s vibrant culture
  • Passion for community, sustainability, and culinary education

"Cooking is my love language. Every dish I create is a celebration of my heritage and a gift to those I serve."

– Siphosethu Krakra
